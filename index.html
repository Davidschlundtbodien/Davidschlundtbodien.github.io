Software Development

Creating, designing, and optimizing are some of my biggest passions of which I aim to use every day. Software development streamlines that creativity into productivity and potential career paths. More

Distribution

What started as a hobby in Craft beer while I worked in alcohol retail turned into passion once I started in Distribution. I was lucky to take part in the growth and development of the Craft beer department at Vin Sauvage. While working in both administration and the warehouse I did my best to expand the knowledge as well as the availability of Craft beer. Mainly through assisting to bring rare and artisanal brands to the Las Vegas market, growing our brand and the breweries we brought to the Valley.

Hobbies & Passions

Brewing

Homebrewing has been a hobby ever since I was introduced to craft beer. I love the challenge of creating traditional styles as well as the freedom to experiment with any part of the process.

Gardening

The main reason I garden is to grow my food. Doing so in the desert is a challenge but I welcome it because I find it fulfilling to watch something thrive over time through hard work. Also, gardening plays into one of my strengths being patience.

Craft Beer

The community and culture behind the beer, in general, is unique and often is a common ground for many people regardless of background. Hiking & Camping

We often forget the amount of noise that surrounds us every day in a city. Being outdoors provides that reset and sanctuary from distractions of modern life. I take that time to center my mind while exploring a new trail or setting up camp.
